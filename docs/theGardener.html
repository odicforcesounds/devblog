The Gardener

A "Perfect Garden", where every flower is in the perfect condition, treat it with care. A lot of flowers with a lot of colors with different shapes, some of them very small some others really big, colorful and full of vitality. I remember to be amazed, fearing to touch any of them, just because they were so perfect that harmonize my soul and I felt that this flowers were better than Gold.

The Gardener was the Dragon and I was just that Monkey who could play there, with total freedom to jump and run, forgetting how much work this garden ask to the gardener who takes the control of it, with all the care is needed to create an Harmony with all that complexity.

There was a Big Cat always around, which was the Tiger, the Guardian of this garden that always like to play with me
