The Process of Transformation

Just another Project to develop. Just another idea to understand and plan, thinking about it. Just another client, just another amount of money. Each client is unique, and you in the Business area, should think like that.

You may wish to understand that ideas, are not predefined UIs and You may need to understand client Budget and Real conditions of possibility for the application Functionality, before starting Developement Process. For example, a client can wish to create an application where the user can teleport itself to some place in the world. You know that molecules can be teleported, but the condition is to have more Hard Drives Space than all Hard-Drives we have today, just to store the information of that body that is unique. So, the condition to archive the Gold is impossible, but you can think how an alternative is possible for the main propose of the client vision. For this specific example, a Video-Meeting is the perfect solution to the main problem of this application.

Since for You (Industry) is mainly for Money, You need to define the possibilities that this application can have, while understanding the main propose of client needs. Client care about statistics and clicks? Client aims to create a payed product or Free? Client is a single person or a Company? Client care about details that you don't care? Client don't care about details that you care? Client wish to understand the logic behind? Client do not care about the configurations of servers and domains??

For all these questions and more, You need to Talk (by listening)

making Questions to your Client

If You have Time to think, then You can read two or more times, all project details that were defined or not, by client vision, but since you can create a meeting, you may wish to ask client to record the meeting. You can re-listening again, to understand better what you didn't understand just by reading. If you do it with your Co-workers, you will find that there are different interpretations and all details are important for that Brain Storming Excercise in that logic process of Understanding the Application Functionality, from what is the gold and what is possible.
