In Portugal, the Olders eat a soup called: "Sopa de Cavalo Cansado", which is "Soup of Tired Horse". (Wine with Bread - Right in The Morning)

It was for many centuries a way to deal with Farming Work where the Hit was High in the morning. To be able to have some Positive Spirit to deal with that Hard work, people drink this "Soup" in the morning, before working. With a Smile in their faces, positive spirit, they work hard.
